Borrowing Services

Bugema University Library offers a range of borrowing services to support your academic needs. Registered students and faculty can borrow books and other materials for a specified period.

  • Loan periods vary depending on the type of material and your user status.
  • Renewals may be possible, subject to availability and library policies.
  • Overdue fines may apply for materials returned after the due date.

Reference Services

Our dedicated library staff are available to assist you with your research and information needs. We can help you:

  • Locate relevant resources for your assignments and research projects.
  • Use the library catalog and other research databases effectively.
  • Understand citation styles and avoid plagiarism.
  • Answer your general inquiries about the library and its resources.

Visit the reference desk during our opening hours or contact us via email or phone.

Interlibrary Loan

If the material you need is not available in our collection, we can often obtain it from other libraries through our interlibrary loan service. Please inquire at the reference desk for more information and to submit a request.

Digital Resources

Access a wide array of digital resources, including e-books, e-journals, and online databases, accessible both on and off-campus. Check our catalog for available online resources and login instructions.

  • Explore academic databases relevant to your field of study.
  • Read full-text articles from scholarly journals.
  • Access online encyclopedias and other reference materials.

Study Spaces and Facilities

The library provides various study spaces to accommodate different learning preferences, including individual study carrels and group study rooms. We also offer:

  • Wi-Fi access for your personal devices.
  • Printing and photocopying services.
  • Computer workstations with internet access and essential software.
