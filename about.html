Our History

The Bugema University Library has been an integral part of the university since its inception. Over the years, it has grown from a small collection to a comprehensive resource center supporting the diverse academic disciplines offered at the university. We are committed to providing access to quality information and fostering a culture of learning and research.

Our Mission

To empower the Bugema University community with the knowledge and resources necessary for academic success, research excellence, and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic library in Uganda, recognized for its innovative services, comprehensive collections, and its role in advancing the intellectual pursuits of Bugema University.
